Germina is looking to make sustainable practices the norm by expanding the sustainable agricultural industry through their projects in vermicompost and black soldier fly breeding.

Issue

In Turkey and around the world, water is used abundantly to deal with agricultural waste. Germina can cut this down by over 50% by using methods of vermicompost and black soldier fly decomposition. Check out our team and projects to see how we are making a change in the world.
